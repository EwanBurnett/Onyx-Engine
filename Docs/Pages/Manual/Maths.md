Maths {#Maths}
===

  The distance between \f$(x_1,y_1)\f$ and \f$(x_2,y_2)\f$ is 
  \f$\sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2}\f$.

## Coordinate System
- Left-handed Cartesian Coordinate system
    - Z+ goes into the screen, Z- comes out of it. 
    - X+ is Right, X- is Left
    - Y+ is Up, Y- is Down
